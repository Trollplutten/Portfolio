Dyson
Motorhead

The Concept

This was the first technical animation I made, and I had a very clear vision from the start. I wanted it to feel like a Dyson, with technical and futuristic elements. But also minimal and clean.

Technical Specs

  • Software: Blender , Fusion360 , Substance sampler , After effects
  • Time: 2 weeks
  • Year: 2026

Motion Preview

The motionpreview was made in a day, I wanted my workflow to be faster and quickly storyboarded with help of a moodboard to speed the process.

The Model

The 3D model was fully modeled in Fusion360. Which then was transferred to Blender for animation and texturing. The Dyson vaccum mostly has plastic materials, which I made in Substance sampler.
